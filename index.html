About

Introspect is a teaching tool to help you learn how to interpret tarot cards with Trungle's tarot deck. When you're ready to add a post, a tarot card is randomly picked from the deck, and you can describe your interpretation of the meaning of the card in your current life circumstances. If you need guidance on how to interpret a card, you can visit the interpret cards section of the site...
